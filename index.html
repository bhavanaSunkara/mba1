📘 MBA Group 1 - Semester 1 Syllabus

The curriculum emphasizes foundational business knowledge in management, finance, economics, and organizational behavior. It aims to develop critical thinking, leadership skills, and a strong understanding of modern business practices for strategic decision-making.

  • Perspectives of Management
  • Managerial Economics
  • Accounting for Management
  • Organizational Behavior
  • Managerial Communication Skills
  • Business Environment
