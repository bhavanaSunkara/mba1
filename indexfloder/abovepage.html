About Our Esteemed Institution

Sarojini Devi College of Information Technology (co-education) was established in 2001 with the MCA course.

Initially affiliated to Andhra University, SDCIT later affiliated to Adikavi Nannaya University in 2016. The college is managed by the Sri Venkata Krishna Educational Society, a purely service-oriented organization dedicated to catering to the education needs of the rural and semi-urban student community.

SDCIT boasts state-of-the-art infrastructure and comprehensive curricular and co-curricular facilities, providing a conducive environment for student growth. Our institution is proud to have excellent experienced faculty who relentlessly strive for the overall development of every student on campus, ensuring academic rigor and personal enrichment.
